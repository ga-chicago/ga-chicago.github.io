Veganism 101

What does it mean to be vegan?

  • No meat
  • No dairy
  • No animal products

What does it NOT mean?

  • Eating leaves...
  • Sad food
  • Being hungry

Where do you get your protein?

  • Soy
  • Nuts, Nut Butters, Seeds
